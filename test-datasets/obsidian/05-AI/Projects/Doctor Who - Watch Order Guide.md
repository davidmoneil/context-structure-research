## Quick Reference

### Regeneration Episodes (Don't Skip!)
| Episode | Transition |
|---------|------------|
| S01E13 - The Parting of the Ways | 9th -> 10th |
| S00E11 - The End of Time Part 2 | 10th -> 11th |
| S00E16 - The Time of the Doctor | 11th -> 12th |
| S00E12 - Twice Upon a Time | 12th -> 13th |
| S00E20 - The Power of the Doctor | 13th -> 14th |
| S00E23 - The Giggle | 14th -> 15th (bi-generation) |

### Must-Watch Specials
- **The Day of the Doctor** - 50th Anniversary, all Doctors
- **The Giggle** - 60th Anniversary, explains bi-generation
- **The End of Time** - Epic Tennant farewell
- **Heaven Sent** - Widely considered best episode ever
