# Quickly master marketing buzzwords and strengthen your SIEM needs

Common Buzz words used today in the cyber security industry around Security Information and Event Management or "SIEM" technologies.  

Link to Similar Post

## Phases of data within your SIEM technology

- **Ingestion **- How does it get your data? 
- **Retention **- How long does it keep your data? Compliance Requirements
- **Parsing **- Can it understand the log and event data you are sending? 
- **Corrolation **- Is it building device / user / credential identities? Also includes a lot of discoverability around managed and unmanaged applications (can be based on integrations). 
- **Context **- Is it looking up IP whois, user data from AD, etc... can also be Threat Intel feed integration (open source or vendor specific). 
- **Integrations **- Integrations can support, Context, Corrolation, UseCase and Incident Response. 
- **Use Cases **- What is it actually detecting from the log data.  This doesn't include the 
- **Incident Response **- Does it assist in the triage and incident management? 
- **Workflow **- Does it mapp against the existing business and IR workflows 
- **Automation **- Does it allow me to automate any of the above areas? 
- **Tracking **- Metrics and reporting maturity? Compliance?
