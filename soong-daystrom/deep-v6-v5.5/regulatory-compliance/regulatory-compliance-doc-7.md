## 2. Regulatory Filing Procedures

### 2.1 Pre-Market Filing Requirements

All products undergo standardized pre-market filing processes managed by our Regulatory Affairs Division under the direction of Dr. Maya Chen (CEO) and Marcus Williams (COO).

#### Product Classification Assessment

Products are assessed across six classification dimensions:

1. **Risk Level** (Class I, II, or III)
2. **Geographic Scope** (domestic, regional, global)
3. **Use Case Category** (industrial, medical, consumer, military)
4. **Technology Sensitivity** (standard, restricted, highly restricted)
5. **Data Handling** (non-sensitive, sensitive, biometric)
6. **Autonomous Capability** (non-autonomous, semi-autonomous, fully autonomous)

#### Filing Timeline Standards

| Filing Type | Assessment Period | Review Period | Total Time | Re-filing Required |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Class I Standard | 2 weeks | 4 weeks | 6 weeks | Every 3 years |
| Class II Standard | 4 weeks | 8 weeks | 12 weeks | Every 2 years |
| Class III (Novel) | 8 weeks | 16 weeks | 24 weeks | Annually |
| Emergency Authorization | 48 hours | 2 weeks | 2 weeks + | As needed |
| International Filing | 6 weeks | 12 weeks | 18 weeks | Per jurisdiction |

## 4. Risk Assessment and Management

### 4.1 Regulatory Risk Classification

Soong-Daystrom uses a standardized risk matrix to assess regulatory exposure:

**Risk Assessment Criteria**

| Dimension | Low | Medium | High | Critical |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Regulatory Complexity** | Single jurisdiction, known rules | Multiple jurisdictions, established rules | Novel jurisdiction or technology | Emerging regulation, untested interpretation |
| **Safety Impact** | Non-critical systems | Limited harm potential | Serious injury possible | Death/permanent disability risk |
| **Market Impact** | <$1M annual revenue | $1-10M revenue | $10-50M revenue | >$50M revenue at risk |
| **Compliance History** | 100% prior compliance | 95%+ compliance | 85-95% compliance | <85% compliance in category |
| **Enforcement Pattern** | Rare enforcement | Occasional fines | Regular enforcement | Known to pursue criminal liability |

**Current Risk Portfolio (2124)**
