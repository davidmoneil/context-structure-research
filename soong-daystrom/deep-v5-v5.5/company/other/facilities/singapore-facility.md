**Strategic Importance:**
- Only SDI facility certified for implantable medical device production
- Houses proprietary NIM assembly and testing capabilities

### 4.2 Manufacturing Process Flow

```
Stage 1: Substrate Preparation (Floor 5)
   ├── Wafer incoming inspection
   ├── Cleaning and surface activation
   ├── Photolithography (outsourced base patterns)
   └── Quality gate: Visual inspection, thickness measurement

Stage 2: Electrode Fabrication (Floor 5)
   ├── Biocompatible coating deposition
   ├── Electrode array patterning
   ├── Surface treatment
   └── Quality gate: Coating thickness, adhesion test

Stage 3: Sub-Assembly (Floor 5)
   ├── Component mounting
   ├── Interconnect fabrication
   ├── Initial electrical test
   └── Quality gate: Continuity, isolation test

Stage 4: Wire Bonding (Floor 6)
   ├── Gold wire bonding
   ├── Bond pull testing (sample)
   ├── Visual inspection
   └── Quality gate: Bond strength, placement accuracy

Stage 5: Final Assembly (Floor 6)
   ├── Housing assembly
   ├── Connector integration
   ├── Sealing preparation
   └── Quality gate: Dimensional verification

Stage 6: Hermetic Sealing (Floor 6)
   ├── Laser welding/glass-to-metal seal
   ├── Gross leak test (100%)
   ├── Fine leak test (100%)
   └── Quality gate: Helium leak rate <1×10⁻⁹ atm·cc/sec

Stage 7: Final Test (Floor 6)
   ├── Full electrical characterization
   ├── Burn-in (48 hours)
   ├── Functional test
   └── Quality gate: All parameters within specification

Stage 8: Packaging (Floor 6)
   ├── Sterile packaging
   ├── Labeling
   ├── Documentation
   └── Quality gate: Package integrity, label accuracy

Stage 9: Sterilization (Outsourced)
   ├── Gamma irradiation (25-40 kGy)
   ├── Sterility testing (sample)
   └── Quality gate: Sterility assurance level (SAL) 10⁻⁶
```

### 4.3 Production Capacity Details
