## Executive Summary

Soong-Daystrom Industries operates in 67 countries, each with distinct regulatory requirements for AI systems, robotics, medical devices, data privacy, and international trade. This document provides comprehensive documentation of country-specific regulatory requirements, certification status, and local partnership arrangements necessary for market access.

The regulatory landscape for advanced robotics and neural interfaces continues to evolve rapidly, with 23 countries introducing new AI-specific legislation in FY2124 alone. SDI's proactive compliance approach—achieving certification ahead of regulatory deadlines—provides competitive advantage in market access timing.
