## Overview

Soong-Daystrom Industries Customer Service supports millions of customers globally with their PCS companions, IAP industrial systems, and NIM medical devices. Our mission is to ensure every customer has an exceptional experience throughout their entire journey with SDI products.

### Service Philosophy

**Core Principles**:
1. **Customer First**: Every decision starts with customer impact
2. **Empathy-Led**: We understand the emotional connection to our products
3. **Knowledge-Powered**: Deep product expertise enables better support
4. **Resolution-Focused**: We solve problems, not just answer questions
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Every interaction teaches us something

## Support Tiers

### Consumer Support

**Tier 1 - General Support**:
- Product questions and guidance
- Troubleshooting basic issues
- Account and warranty management
- Order status and returns
- First call resolution target: 75%

**Tier 2 - Technical Support**:
- Complex troubleshooting
- Software issues
- Integration problems
- Remote diagnostics
- Escalation from Tier 1

## Training Program

### New Agent Training

**Duration**: 6 weeks

**Curriculum**:
- Week 1: Company culture, systems, tools
- Week 2: Product knowledge (basics)
- Week 3: Product knowledge (advanced)
- Week 4: Troubleshooting methodology
- Week 5: Customer interaction skills
- Week 6: Supervised call handling

**Certification Requirements**:
- Product knowledge exam (85% pass)
- Systems proficiency test
- Soft skills evaluation
- Supervised call quality (85%+)

### Ongoing Training

| Training | Frequency | Duration |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Product updates | Weekly | 30 min |
| New feature deep-dive | Monthly | 2 hours |
| Soft skills | Quarterly | 4 hours |

| Recertification | Annual | Full day |

**Career Path**:

## Quality Assurance

### Call Monitoring

**Sample Rate**: 5% of all interactions
**Evaluation Criteria**:
- Opening/closing procedure (10%)
- Active listening (20%)
- Problem identification (20%)
- Resolution quality (25%)
- Product knowledge (15%)
- Documentation (10%)

**Calibration**: Weekly sessions with all supervisors

### Customer Feedback

**Post-Interaction Survey**:
- Sent after 100% of interactions
- Response rate: 24%
- Questions: Satisfaction, effort, NPS

**Monthly Customer Panel**:
- 50 customers per region
- Deep-dive discussions
- Product feedback collection
- Service improvement ideas
