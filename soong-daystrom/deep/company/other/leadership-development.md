## Program Philosophy

At Soong-Daystrom Industries, we believe that exceptional leadership is the foundation of sustainable innovation and growth. Our leadership development philosophy is built on three core principles:

1. **Leaders at Every Level**: Leadership is not confined to formal management roles; we develop leadership capabilities across the organization
2. **Grow Our Own**: We prioritize internal development and promotion over external hiring for leadership positions
3. **Continuous Evolution**: Leadership requirements evolve with our technology and organization; our programs adapt accordingly

### Strategic Objectives

| Objective | Target | Current (2124) |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Internal promotion rate (Director+) | 80% | 76% |
| Leadership bench strength ratio | 2.0 | 1.8 |
| Manager effectiveness score | 85% | 82% |
| Succession readiness (critical roles) | 90% | 84% |
| Leadership program ROI | 200% | 187% |

## Leadership Framework

### SDI Leadership Competency Model

All leadership development programs align to the SDI Leadership Competency Model, which defines the behaviors and capabilities expected at each leadership level.

#### Core Competencies (All Levels)

| Competency | Definition |
|------------|------------|
| **Strategic Thinking** | Ability to see the big picture, anticipate trends, and align decisions with long-term objectives |
| **Innovation Leadership** | Fostering creativity, encouraging experimentation, and driving breakthrough solutions |
| **People Development** | Investing in others' growth, providing feedback, and building high-performing teams |
| **Ethical Courage** | Making principled decisions, especially when difficult; modeling integrity |
| **Results Orientation** | Setting ambitious goals and driving accountability for outcomes |
| **Communication Excellence** | Articulating vision, listening actively, and adapting style to audience |
| **Collaboration** | Building partnerships across boundaries, leveraging diverse perspectives |
| **Change Leadership** | Navigating ambiguity, driving transformation, building resilience |

#### Level-Specific Competency Emphasis

| Level | Primary Focus | Development Priority |
|-------|---------------|----------------------|
| Team Lead | People Development, Results | Transition from individual contributor |
| Manager | Team Building, Communication | Developing others, delegation |
| Senior Manager | Strategic Thinking, Change Leadership | Cross-functional influence |
| Director | Innovation Leadership, Collaboration | Organizational impact |
| Vice President | Strategic Thinking, Ethical Courage | Enterprise perspective |
| Executive | All competencies at mastery level | Industry and market leadership |

## Leadership Development Programs

### Emerging Leaders Program (ELP)

**Target Audience**: High-potential individual contributors identified for future management roles
**Duration**: 12 months
**Cohort Size**: 24 participants
**Program Frequency**: Annual (September start)
**Investment**: $8,500 per participant

#### Eligibility Criteria

- 2+ years at SDI
- Performance rating of "Exceeds" or higher for 2 consecutive years
- Manager nomination and director approval
- Assessment center results meeting threshold

#### Program Components

| Component | Duration | Description |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Leadership Foundations | 3 days | Core concepts, SDI leadership model, self-assessment |
| Monthly Workshops | 4 hours/month | Deep dives on specific competencies |
| Executive Mentorship | 12 months | Matched with VP or above mentor |
| Peer Learning Circles | Monthly | Small group problem-solving and support |
| Shadow Assignment | 2 weeks | Observation of senior leader |
| Capstone Project | 4 months | Cross-functional improvement initiative |
| 360-Degree Feedback | Twice | Start and end of program |

#### Program Schedule

| Month | Focus Area | Key Activities |
|-------|------------|----------------|
| 1 | Program Launch | Orientation, 360 feedback, mentor matching |
| 2 | Self-Awareness | Leadership style assessment, personal development plan |
| 3 | Communication | Presentation skills, difficult conversations |
| 4 | Team Dynamics | Building trust, managing conflict |
| 5 | Results Delivery | Goal setting, accountability, performance management basics |
| 6 | Strategic Thinking | Business acumen, strategic planning |
| 7 | Innovation | Creative problem solving, design thinking |
| 8 | Change Leadership | Leading through ambiguity, resilience |
| 9 | Capstone Planning | Project scoping, team formation |
| 10 | Capstone Execution | Implementation and iteration |
| 11 | Capstone Presentation | Executive presentation and feedback |
| 12 | Program Completion | Final 360, celebration, next steps |

#### Outcomes (2123 Cohort)

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|

### New Manager Essentials (NME)

**Target Audience**: Newly promoted or hired managers (within 6 months of transition)
**Duration**: 6 months
**Cohort Size**: 16 participants
**Program Frequency**: Quarterly (January, April, July, October)
**Investment**: $4,200 per participant

#### Eligibility

- All new managers (required within first 6 months)
- First-time people managers or managers new to SDI

#### Program Components

| Component | Duration | Description |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Manager Bootcamp | 3 days | Intensive onboarding to management role |
| Monthly Sessions | 3 hours | Topic-focused workshops |
| Manager Toolkit | Self-paced | Online resources and job aids |
| Peer Cohort | 6 months | Small group support and accountability |
| Manager Coach | 6 sessions | One-on-one coaching from certified coach |
| 90-Day Checkpoint | 2 hours | Review progress, adjust development plan |

#### Curriculum Outline

**Module 1: Manager Bootcamp (Days 1-3)**
- Your Identity as a Manager
- SDI Management Systems and Processes
- Employment Law and HR Compliance
- Performance Management Fundamentals
- Having Effective 1:1 Conversations
- Setting Goals and Expectations
- Giving and Receiving Feedback

**Module 2: Building Your Team (Month 2)**
- Hiring and Onboarding Excellence
- Team Dynamics and Norming
- Delegation and Empowerment
- Managing Remote and Hybrid Teams

**Module 3: Performance and Development (Month 3)**
- Performance Coaching Conversations
- Writing Effective Performance Reviews
- Career Development Discussions
- Addressing Performance Issues

**Module 4: Communication and Influence (Month 4)**
- Communication Styles and Adaptation
- Managing Up: Working with Your Leader
- Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Running Effective Meetings

**Module 5: Navigating Challenges (Month 5)**
- Conflict Resolution
- Managing Change on Your Team
- Time Management and Prioritization
- Stress Management and Resilience

**Module 6: Your Leadership Brand (Month 6)**
- Defining Your Leadership Philosophy
- Building Your Network
- Continuous Development Planning
- Program Completion and Celebration

### Executive Development Program (EDP)

**Target Audience**: Vice Presidents and newly appointed C-suite executives
**Duration**: 24 months
**Cohort Size**: 8-10 participants
**Program Frequency**: Annual (invitation-only)
**Investment**: $125,000 per participant

#### Program Overview

The Executive Development Program prepares senior leaders for enterprise-level impact and potential CEO succession. Participants engage in intensive development focused on strategic leadership, board relations, external visibility, and industry influence.

#### Program Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Executive Assessment** | Comprehensive leadership assessment including Hogan, 360, and executive interviews |
| **Individual Development Plan** | Customized 24-month plan addressing specific development priorities |
| **Executive Coaching** | 48 sessions over 24 months with world-class executive coach |
| **Board Mentorship** | Paired with board member for ongoing dialogue and counsel |
| **Industry Immersion** | Participation in industry forums, policy discussions, standards bodies |
| **Public Profile Development** | Media training, thought leadership platform, keynote speaking |
| **CEO Dialogue Series** | Quarterly private sessions with CEO |
| **External Board Experience** | Supported to join nonprofit or advisory board |
| **Executive Education** | Harvard AMP, Stanford SEP, or equivalent (2 weeks) |
| **Global Rotation** | 4-week assignment at international location |

#### Development Themes

| Quarter | Theme | Key Activities |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| Q1 | Self-Awareness and Authentic Leadership | Assessment, coaching launch, philosophy definition |
| Q2 | Enterprise Strategy | Strategic planning participation, scenario work |
| Q3 | Stakeholder Leadership | Board exposure, investor relations, government affairs |
| Q4 | Organizational Transformation | Leading large-scale change, culture shaping |
| Q5 | External Visibility | Media, conferences, industry relationships |
| Q6 | Innovation at Scale | Portfolio management, venture thinking, M&A |
| Q7 | Talent as Strategic Asset | Succession planning, culture as competitive advantage |
| Q8 | Legacy and Succession | Personal legacy, development of successors, completion |

## Leadership Coaching

### Internal Coaching

SDI maintains a team of 12 certified internal coaches available for leadership development.

| Coach Level | Availability | Typical Clients |
|-------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Associate Coach | All managers | New managers, team leads |
| Senior Coach | Director+ | Directors, senior managers |
| Executive Coach | VP+ | VPs, executives |

**Engagement Model**:
- 6-session packages (standard)
- Focused on specific development goals
- Supervisor awareness (not involvement in content)
- Confidential within ethical boundaries

### External Coaching

External executive coaches are engaged for senior leaders and high-potential development.

**Approved Coach Network**: 24 certified executive coaches
**Credential Requirements**: ICF PCC or above, 10+ years executive experience
**Typical Engagement**: 12-24 months, twice-monthly sessions

## Leadership Assessment

### Assessment Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Used For |
|------|---------|----------|
| Hogan Leadership Series | Personality, derailers, values | Manager+, selection |
| 360-Degree Feedback | Multi-rater behavioral feedback | All levels, development |
| SDI Leadership Assessment | Custom competency assessment | Promotion readiness |
| Cognitive Ability | Problem-solving, learning agility | Selection, high-potential |
| Simulation/Assessment Center | Behavioral demonstration | ELP selection, promotions |

### Assessment Center Process

Used for Emerging Leaders Program selection and senior promotions:

**Duration**: Full day
**Activities**:
- Case study analysis and presentation
- Group problem-solving exercise
- Role play (coaching conversation, stakeholder management)
- In-basket exercise
- Panel interview

**Assessors**: Trained internal assessors (Directors+) plus external I/O psychologist
