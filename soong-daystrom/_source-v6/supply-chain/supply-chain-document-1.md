## Current State Assessment

### Supply Chain Management

As of 2122, SDI's supply chain management processes have undergone significant changes due to increased global demand and supply disruptions. Key statistics include:

* Average lead time: 12 weeks (up from 8 weeks in 2115)
* Stockout rate: 3.4% (down from 5.1% in 2117)
* Inventory turnover: 14 times per annum

Despite these improvements, our supply chain remains vulnerable to fluctuations in global commodity prices and regional disruptions.

### Manufacturing Operations

Our manufacturing facilities are spread across multiple locations worldwide, with a total capacity of 250,000 units per annum. Key metrics include:

* Production efficiency: 80% (up from 75% in 2119)
* Quality control rate: 99.5%
* Employee utilization: 92%

However, recent capacity constraints have resulted in increased overtime hours and reduced production flexibility.

## Strategic Objectives

Based on our analysis, we propose the following strategic objectives for enhancing supply chain and manufacturing operations:

### Supply Chain Management

1. Implement a more flexible and responsive procurement strategy to mitigate the impact of global commodity price fluctuations.
2. Invest in digitalization technologies (e.g., blockchain, IoT) to enhance data accuracy and enable real-time monitoring of inventory levels.
3. Strengthen partnerships with key suppliers to ensure reliable delivery and reduce lead times.

### Manufacturing Operations

1. Implement a Total Productive Maintenance (TPM) program to improve equipment reliability and reduce downtime.
2. Invest in automation technologies (e.g., robotics, machine learning) to enhance production efficiency and quality control.
3. Develop a more flexible and adaptive manufacturing schedule to accommodate changes in demand and supply.

## Project Prometheus

Project Prometheus aims to integrate AI and robotics into our manufacturing operation to improve efficiency and productivity. Key objectives include:

* Implement AI-powered predictive maintenance to reduce equipment downtime by 30%.
* Introduce robotic process automation (RPA) to increase production capacity by 25%.

We expect the successful implementation of Prometheus to result in significant cost savings, improved product quality, and enhanced competitiveness.
