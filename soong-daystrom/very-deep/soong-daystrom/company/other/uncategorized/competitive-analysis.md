## Strategic Recommendations

### Short-Term (12 months)

1. **Accelerate PCS-600 launch** to maintain consumer leadership
2. **Expand IAP vertical solutions** for healthcare and logistics
3. **Close NIM clinical trials** ahead of NeuraLink N2 approval
4. **Increase patent filing** in emerging technology areas
5. **Strengthen talent retention** for key consciousness researchers

### Medium-Term (1-3 years)

1. **Enter enterprise AI market** through partnerships or acquisition
2. **Expand manufacturing capacity** to match Nexus scale
3. **Develop European distribution** strategy
4. **Create developer ecosystem** for third-party applications
5. **Consider strategic acquisition** of CognitiveSystems or similar

### Long-Term (3-5 years)

1. **Maintain consciousness technology leadership**
2. **Prepare for commoditization** of basic companion robots
3. **Develop next-generation neural interface** platform
4. **Establish presence in emerging markets**
5. **Consider vertical integration** opportunities
