## 4. Risk Assessment and Mitigation

### 4.1 Enterprise Risk Classification

Soong-Daystrom employs a four-tier risk classification system for regulatory and compliance matters:

**Tier 1 - Critical Risk:** Potential for material regulatory violation, loss of certification, or significant financial/reputational impact.

**Tier 2 - High Risk:** Substantial compliance gap with moderate likelihood and significant impact; requires immediate remediation.

**Tier 3 - Medium Risk:** Compliance vulnerabilities with manageable impact; requires monitoring and planned remediation.

**Tier 4 - Low Risk:** Minor compliance issues with minimal impact; routine remediation schedule.
