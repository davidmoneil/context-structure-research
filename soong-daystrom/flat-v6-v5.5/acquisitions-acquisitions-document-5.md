NovaTech is a leading provider of autonomous vehicle systems, with a focus on urban mobility solutions. Their technology has shown promising results in reducing congestion and increasing efficiency in crowded cities.

**Due Diligence Findings:**

* NovaTech's Autopilot System (APS) demonstrated a 95% accuracy rate in complex scenarios, outperforming industry benchmarks.
* The company's proprietary sensor suite has been recognized for its exceptional performance in harsh environments.
* NovaTech's business model is well-positioned for rapid expansion into new markets.

**Integration Feasibility:**
We believe that integrating NovaTech's technology with our existing IAP Platform will enhance our AI capabilities and provide a significant competitive advantage. Our R&D team is already exploring ways to integrate APS with the IAP, with preliminary results showing promising synergies.

**Valuation Assessment:**
Based on our due diligence, we estimate a valuation of $850 million for NovaTech Inc., with a potential acquisition multiple of 4x EBITDA.

**III. Target 2: Aurora Innovations**

Aurora is a pioneering developer of neural interfaces (NIs) for human-machine interaction. Their technology has the potential to revolutionize industries such as healthcare, education, and entertainment.

**Due Diligence Findings:**

* Aurora's NI system demonstrated exceptional accuracy in reading brain signals, with an average error rate of 0.5%.
* The company's proprietary software framework provides a flexible and scalable platform for developers.
* Aurora's team has made significant progress in addressing key technical challenges, including signal processing and data security.

**Integration Feasibility:**
We envision integrating Aurora's NI technology with our existing products, such as the PCS-9000 neural interface. Our CTO has already begun exploring ways to leverage Aurora's expertise in developing new applications for our existing technology.

**Valuation Assessment:**
Based on our due diligence, we estimate a valuation of $600 million for Aurora Innovations, with a potential acquisition multiple of 3x EBITDA.
