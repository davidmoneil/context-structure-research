### 2.2 Case Study: PCS-9000 Assembly Optimization

The PCS-9000 robotics platform is our most complex product, requiring assembly of 487 distinct components with multiple integration checkpoints. Prometheus analysis identified that existing scheduling allocated resources based on historical averages rather than real-time production state.

**Before Optimization (Q3 2122):**
- Average assembly cycle: 14.2 hours per unit
- Unplanned downtime: 4.2 hours/week
- Quality control bottleneck: 2.1 hours per unit average
- First-pass yield rate: 91.4%

**After Optimization (Q1 2123):**
- Average assembly cycle: 11.6 hours per unit (18.3% improvement)
- Unplanned downtime: 1.1 hours/week (73.8% reduction)
- Quality control time: 1.1 hours per unit (47.6% improvement)
- First-pass yield rate: 96.8%

The Prometheus system continuously rebalances workstation assignments, predicts component delivery timing, and alerts supervisory staff to potential bottlenecks 2-3 hours in advance. This enabled a 22% increase in monthly PCS-9000 output (from 847 units to 1,034 units) without additional capital expenditure.

## 11. Challenges & Lessons Learned

### 11.1 Implementation Challenges

Despite overall success, the improvement initiatives encountered specific challenges:

**Challenge 1: Change Management Resistance**
- Issue: Initial resistance from experienced operators to new processes
- Resolution: Expanded training program and created "power user" mentors
- Outcome: Full adoption achieved in 6 months

**Challenge 2: Technology Integration Complexity**
- Issue: Prometheus AI systems required 8 weeks of calibration with manufacturing data
- Resolution: Dedicated integration team and extended go-live timeline
- Outcome: System now operating at design specifications

**Challenge 3: Supply Chain Coordination**
- Issue: Supplier consolidation initially created single-source dependency risks
- Resolution: Implemented secondary supplier relationships for critical components
- Outcome: Risk mitigated while maintaining cost benefits

### 11.2 Key Lessons

1. **Executive Alignment Essential:** Success required direct CEO and COO sponsorship; initiatives with unclear senior leadership support faltered
2. **Data Quality Critical:** Prometheus AI effectiveness directly correlated with historical data accuracy
3. **Workforce Engagement Matters:** Facilities with higher employee engagement achieved 23% better improvement outcomes
4. **Iterative Deployment Better:** Phased rollouts outperformed "big bang" implementations by 3.2x
5. **Cross-functional Teams Drive Results:** Projects with manufacturing, engineering, and operations team inclusion achieved targets 18% faster
