# Internal Corporate Document
## Soong-Daystrom Industries
### International Operations and Global Expansion (Part 3)

**Executive Summary**

As we continue to solidify our position as a leader in the global technology industry, it is essential that we expand our operations to new markets and establish ourselves as a dominant force in the international arena. This document outlines our strategy for achieving this goal over the next five years, from 2120 to 2125.

**Current Status**

As of December 2022, Soong-Daystrom Industries has established a strong presence in three continents: North America, Europe, and Asia. Our product portfolio includes the PCS-9000, NIM-7, and IAP, which have gained significant traction in the market. We have also initiated several projects aimed at expanding our capabilities and staying ahead of the curve.

**Strategic Objectives**

1. **Establish a strong foothold in Asia**
	* Expand manufacturing facilities to meet growing demand for PCS-9000 and NIM-7
	* Establish strategic partnerships with local suppliers and distributors
	* Achieve $500 million in revenue from Asian markets by 2125
2. **Enter the European market**
	* Launch IAP product line in major European cities by 2123
	* Establish a network of sales and support offices across Europe
	* Achieve $750 million in revenue from European markets by 2125
3. **Establish a presence in North America**
	* Expand manufacturing facilities to meet growing demand for PCS-9000 and NIM-7
	* Establish strategic partnerships with local suppliers and distributors
	* Achieve $1 billion in revenue from North American markets by 2125
