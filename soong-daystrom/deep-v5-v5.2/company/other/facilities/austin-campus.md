## 1. Campus Overview

### 1.1 Location and History

The Austin Campus serves as Soong-Daystrom Industries' primary manufacturing and assembly facility, producing the full range of industrial and service robotics platforms. Located in the Austin Technology Corridor, the campus benefits from proximity to major universities, a skilled workforce, and excellent logistics infrastructure.

## 5. Infrastructure

### 5.1 Electrical System

**Utility Service:**
- Provider: Austin Energy
- Service: 138kV transmission, stepped to 13.8kV distribution
- Capacity: 45 MVA (2 feeders, automatic transfer)
- Redundancy: N+1 feeder configuration

**Emergency Power:**
- Generators: 4 x 2.5 MW diesel generators (Building 9)
- UPS: 3 x 750 kVA for critical systems
- Transfer Time: <10 seconds (automatic)
- Fuel Storage: 72-hour capacity

**Distribution:**
- Primary substations: 4 (13.8kV to 480V)
- Secondary distribution: 480V, 208V, 120V
- Critical loads on UPS: Data centers, cleanroom, safety systems

### 5.2 HVAC Systems

**Central Plant (Building 9):**
- Chillers: 3 x 250-ton centrifugal (N+1)
- Cooling Towers: 3 cells, 850 tons total
- Boilers: 2 x 150 HP (natural gas)

**Building-Specific:**
- Manufacturing halls: Rooftop units, spot cooling
- Building 7 cleanroom: Dedicated AHUs with HEPA filtration
- Engineering labs: Precision temperature control (±1°F)

### 5.3 Compressed Air

**Generation:**
- Compressors: 4 x 300 HP oil-free (Atlas Copco)
- Capacity: 4,800 CFM total
- Pressure: 125 PSI (plant), 90 PSI (point of use)
- Drying: Desiccant dryers, -40°F dewpoint

**Distribution:**
- Loop system with isolation valves
- Pressure monitoring throughout
- Emergency backup: 2 portable compressors (rented standby agreement)

### 5.4 Water Systems

**Domestic Water:**
- Source: City of Austin
- Storage: 200,000 gallon elevated tank
- Fire suppression: Dedicated mains, 500,000 gallon reserve

**Process Water:**
- DI Water: 2 x 50 GPM systems (Building 5, 7)
- Cooling Water: Closed loop, glycol-treated
- Wastewater: On-site pretreatment before discharge

### 5.5 Data/Communications

**Network:**
- Redundant fiber connections (AT&T, Spectrum Enterprise)
- Campus fiber backbone (10 Gbps)
- Building distribution: Cat 6A, wireless coverage
- Data center: Building 2 (primary), Building 1 (DR)

**Manufacturing Systems:**
- Dedicated OT network (isolated from IT)
- MES connectivity to all production equipment
- Industrial wireless for mobile devices
