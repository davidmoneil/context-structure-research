## Partnership Strategy Overview

Soong-Daystrom Industries maintains strategic partnerships across four categories: technology, distribution, research, and healthcare. These partnerships extend our capabilities, accelerate market access, and enhance our innovation pipeline.

### Partnership Philosophy

1. **Mutual Value Creation**: Both parties must benefit meaningfully
2. **Strategic Alignment**: Partners share our commitment to ethical AI and beneficial technology
3. **Long-term Orientation**: We prefer multi-year relationships over transactional arrangements
4. **Open Innovation**: We believe collaboration accelerates progress for the entire industry

## Partnership Governance

### Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Criteria | Governance | Review Frequency |
|------|----------|------------|------------------|
| Strategic | >$100M annual value | Executive sponsor, quarterly steering | Quarterly |
| Major | $25-100M annual value | VP sponsor, semi-annual review | Semi-annually |
| Standard | $5-25M annual value | Director sponsor, annual review | Annually |
| Emerging | <$5M annual value | Manager sponsor | As needed |

### Partnership Lifecycle

1. **Identification**: Strategic fit assessment
2. **Evaluation**: Due diligence, pilot program
3. **Negotiation**: Terms, IP, exclusivity
4. **Launch**: Integration, enablement, announcement
5. **Optimization**: Performance improvement
6. **Renewal/Exit**: Strategic reassessment

### Governance Committees

**Partnership Advisory Board**:
- Meets: Quarterly
- Composition: CEO, CTO, CMO, CFO, CLO
- Purpose: Strategic direction, major decisions

**Partnership Operations Committee**:
- Meets: Monthly
- Composition: VP-level partnership leads
- Purpose: Operational issues, resource allocation
