# International Operations and Global Expansion: A Strategic Roadmap for Soong-Daystrom Industries (2120-2125)

## Executive Summary

As we approach the midpoint of our ambitious global expansion strategy, it is essential to reassess our progress, identify areas for improvement, and outline a revised roadmap for continued growth. This document provides an overview of our international operations and highlights key milestones achieved between 2020 and 2125.

### Departmental Overview

Our global presence is supported by four primary departments:

1.  **International Sales**: Responsible for expanding our customer base in emerging markets.
2.  **Research and Development**: Oversees the development of new products and technologies.
3.  **Manufacturing and Logistics**: Coordinates production, distribution, and supply chain management worldwide.
