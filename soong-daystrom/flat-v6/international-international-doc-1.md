# Soong-Daystrom Industries: International Operations and Global Expansion (Part 1)

Soong-Daystrom Industries has embarked on a strategic initiative to expand its global presence and solidify its position as a leading technology company worldwide. This document outlines the key milestones, achievements, and future plans for our international operations from 2120 to 2125.

## Market Analysis and Objectives

In 2120, Soong-Daystrom Industries identified emerging markets in Asia and Latin America as prime targets for expansion. Our primary objectives were:

* Establish a strong presence in these regions
* Increase market share by at least 20% within the next five years
* Introduce our flagship products, PCS-9000, NIM-7, and IAP, to target markets

## 2120: Initial Expansion Efforts

In January 2020, we established a subsidiary in Tokyo, Japan, marking our entry into the Asian market. This move was facilitated by Dr. Maya Chen, CEO, who recognized the vast potential of the region for growth.

## 2121: Prometheus Project Launch

In April 2021, we launched the Prometheus project, a comprehensive overhaul of our supply chain and logistics infrastructure. This initiative aimed to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance customer satisfaction.

Key Highlights:

* Increased manufacturing capacity by 30%
* Improved delivery times by 25%
* Reduced inventory levels by 20%

## 2122: Atlas Project Completion

In October 2022, we completed the Atlas project, a major technological advancement in our artificial intelligence department. This achievement enabled us to develop more sophisticated AI solutions for our customers.

Key Highlights:

## 2123: Hermes Project Launch
