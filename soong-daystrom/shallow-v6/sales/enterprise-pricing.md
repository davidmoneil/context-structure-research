## Executive Summary

This document outlines Soong-Daystrom Industries' enterprise pricing framework for fiscal year 2125. Our pricing strategy balances competitive positioning with value-based pricing principles, ensuring we capture appropriate value for our industry-leading positronic systems while maintaining accessibility across market segments.

Our enterprise pricing model is built on three pillars:
1. **Unit-based hardware pricing** with volume incentives
2. **Subscription-based software licensing** for cognitive matrices and updates
3. **Tiered support agreements** scaled to operational criticality

## Software Licensing Models

### Cognitive Matrix Operating System (CMOS)

All positronic units require CMOS licensing. Three models available:

#### Perpetual License

| Tier | Per-Unit Cost | Annual Maintenance | Features |
|------|---------------|-------------------|----------|
| CMOS Basic | 180,000 | 36,000 (20%) | Core cognitive functions |
| CMOS Professional | 340,000 | 68,000 (20%) | Enhanced learning, multi-task |
| CMOS Enterprise | 580,000 | 87,000 (15%) | Full capability, priority updates |

**Perpetual License Terms:**
- License grants usage rights for hardware lifetime
- Maintenance mandatory for continued operation (regulatory requirement)
- Maintenance includes security patches, regulatory compliance updates
- Feature updates require separate purchase or maintenance upgrade

#### Subscription License

| Tier | Monthly Per-Unit | Annual Per-Unit | Included |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|----------|
| CMOS Basic | 4,500 | 48,600 | Core functions + all updates |
| CMOS Professional | 8,200 | 88,560 | Enhanced + priority support |
| CMOS Enterprise | 14,800 | 159,840 | Full capability + early access |

**Subscription Benefits:**
- All updates included (security, feature, regulatory)
- Flexible unit count (monthly adjustment for fleet changes)
- No upfront capital expenditure
- Automatic compliance maintenance

#### Consumption-Based License

For variable or project-based deployments:

| Metric | Rate | Minimum | Maximum |
|--------|------|---------|---------|
| Cognitive Cycles (billion) | 0.85 credits | 10M cycles/month | No cap |
| Active Operating Hours | 12 credits/hour | 100 hours/month | 720 hours/month |
| Task Complexity Units | 0.45 credits | 500 TCU/month | No cap |

**Consumption Model Conditions:**
- Usage metering via SDI CloudBrain connection (mandatory)
- Monthly billing in arrears
- 15% premium over equivalent subscription for variable workloads
- Not available for autonomous or offline deployments

## Support Tier Definitions

### Standard Support (Included)

All enterprise purchases include baseline support:

- **Response Time:** 4 business hours for Critical, 8 hours for High
- **Availability:** Monday-Friday, 0600-2200 customer local time
- **Channels:** Portal, email, comm-link
- **Coverage:** Software issues, basic troubleshooting
- **Exclusions:** On-site support, hardware repair, customization assistance

### Premium Support Tiers

| Feature | Silver | Gold | Platinum | Diamond |
|---------|--------|------|----------|---------|
| **Annual Cost** | 8% of license value | 15% of license value | 22% of license value | Custom |
| **Critical Response** | 2 hours | 1 hour | 30 minutes | 15 minutes |
| **High Response** | 4 hours | 2 hours | 1 hour | 30 minutes |
| **Availability** | 18x6 | 24x6 | 24x7 | 24x7x365 |
| **On-Site Support** | 4 visits/year | 12 visits/year | Unlimited | Dedicated tech |
| **Cognitive Specialist Access** | Portal only | Scheduled calls | Priority access | Named specialist |
| **Hardware Repair SLA** | 72 hours | 48 hours | 24 hours | 8 hours |
| **Spare Units Pool** | No | No | Regional | On-site |
| **Proactive Monitoring** | No | Basic | Advanced | Predictive |
| **Training Credits** | 10,000 | 25,000 | 75,000 | 150,000 |

### Mission-Critical Support Program

For deployments where downtime has severe operational or safety implications:

**Qualification Requirements:**
- Minimum 25 deployed units
- Documented criticality assessment
- Approved contingency procedures
- Annual recertification

**Program Features:**
- Guaranteed 99.97% uptime SLA
- Financial penalties for SLA breaches (up to 25% monthly support fees)
- Dedicated support pod (3-5 specialists)
- Real-time cognitive monitoring and intervention
- Quarterly operational reviews
- Priority access to replacement units
- Annual cost: 35% of total fleet license value (minimum 500,000 credits)
