## IV. Risk Assessment Framework

### A. Enterprise Risk Classification System

Soong-Daystrom employs a five-tier risk assessment framework across all regulatory and operational domains:

| Risk Level | Annual Probability | Potential Impact | Treatment Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Critical (Red) | >40% | >$50M loss or regulatory shutdown | Immediate escalation; board reporting; contingency activation |
| High (Orange) | 25-40% | $10M-$50M loss or significant operational impact | Quarterly monitoring; dedicated mitigation plans |
| Medium (Yellow) | 10-25% | $1M-$10M loss or moderate operational disruption | Regular monitoring; policy controls; training |
| Low (Blue) | 5-10% | <$1M loss or minimal operational impact | Standard monitoring; documented controls |
| Minimal (Green) | <5% | Negligible financial impact | Standard governance processes |
