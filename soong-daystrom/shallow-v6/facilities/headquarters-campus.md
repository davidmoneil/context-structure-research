## Campus Overview

The Soong-Daystrom Industries global headquarters campus spans 47 acres in the South of Market district of San Francisco, California. Originally developed between 2095 and 2102, the campus has undergone three major expansion phases to accommodate the company's growth from a startup to a global technology leader.

- Covered walkways between all buildings

- Energy self-sufficiency: 94%

**HVAC and Climate**:
- Central plant with thermal storage

- Free cooling optimization
- AI-driven load balancing
- LEED Platinum certification (all buildings)

**Systems**:

**Cybersecurity**:
