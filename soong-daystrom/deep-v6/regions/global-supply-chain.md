## Executive Summary

Soong-Daystrom Industries operates a sophisticated global supply chain network spanning 34 countries, 187 tier-1 suppliers, and 12 manufacturing/assembly facilities. The network is designed for resilience, cost efficiency, and regulatory compliance across multiple jurisdictions, supporting annual production of approximately 850,000 units across all product lines.

This document provides comprehensive documentation of SDI's supply chain infrastructure, including supplier relationships, logistics networks, inventory strategy, and risk management frameworks.
