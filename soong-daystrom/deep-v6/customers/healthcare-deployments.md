Apollo Hospitals presented unique implementation challenges:
- Extreme patient volume (5.6 million annually)

- Juliana units programmed with 22 Indian languages

**Tiered Service Model**
- Premium tier: Dedicated android assistance
- Standard tier: Shared android resources
- Subsidized tier: Android-augmented community health
- Result: Quality care across all economic levels

**Rural Telemedicine Network**
