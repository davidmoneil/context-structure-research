**Chief Executive Officer**
Employee ID: SDI-0001
Board Member Since: 2115

Dr. Chen has served as CEO of Soong-Daystrom Industries since January 2115, guiding the company through a period of unprecedented growth that has seen revenue increase from $8.7 billion to $47.2 billion.

**Education**:

**Career History**:

Dr. Chen was recruited to SDI in 2108 by then-CEO Dr. Richard Daystrom to develop the company's long-term strategy. Her strategic vision document, "SDI 2130: From Robots to Relationships," became the blueprint for the company's expansion into companion robotics. As President of North America, she drove the successful launch of the PCS-300 and established SDI's enterprise sales organization.

As CEO, Dr. Chen has balanced aggressive growth with ethical leadership. She championed the creation of SDI's AI Ethics Board in 2116 and established the company's industry-leading safety standards. Under her leadership, SDI became the first robotics company to achieve the Ethics in AI certification from the IEEE.

**Board Positions**:

- World Economic Forum Young Global Leader (2110)
- IEEE Computer Society Women of Vision Award (2118)

**Personal**: Dr. Chen lives in San Francisco with her spouse, Dr. Michael Chen (no relation; Professor of Economics at UC Berkeley), and their two children. She is fluent in Mandarin, English, and Japanese.
