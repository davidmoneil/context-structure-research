## Program Overview

The Soong-Daystrom Industries Channel Partner Program enables qualified organizations to resell, implement, and support SDI positronic systems across designated territories. Our partner ecosystem is fundamental to achieving universal market coverage while maintaining the service excellence our customers expect.

### Program Mission

*"To build an ecosystem of trusted partners who extend SDI's reach and expertise, ensuring every customer receives world-class positronic solutions regardless of location or industry vertical."*

### Program Principles

1. **Mutual Profitability** - Partner success drives SDI success
2. **Customer-Centricity** - End-customer experience is paramount
3. **Technical Excellence** - Only certified partners represent SDI
4. **Territorial Integrity** - Protected markets enable investment
5. **Continuous Development** - Ongoing training and enablement

## Territory Management

### Territory Types

**Exclusive Territory**
- Single partner has sole rights to SDI sales within defined geography
- Requires Elite or Strategic partnership
- Annual revenue minimums enforced
- Territory fees may apply

**Protected Territory**
- Partner receives deal registration protection within defined geography
- Multiple partners may operate, but registered deals protected
- Standard for Select and Premier partners

**Open Territory**
- Multiple partners compete freely
- Deal registration provides limited protection
- Standard for Authorized partners

## Deal Registration

### Process Overview

1. Partner submits deal registration via Partner Portal
2. SDI validates customer and opportunity
3. Approval/rejection within 48 business hours
4. Approved deals receive margin protection for registration period
5. Partner has exclusive access to registered opportunity

### Registration Requirements

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Customer Name | Yes | Legal entity name |
| Customer Contact | Yes | Primary decision maker |
| Opportunity Size | Yes | Estimated credits |
| Products | Yes | Specific SKUs or categories |
| Estimated Close | Yes | Quarter minimum |
| Competitive Situation | No | Helpful for support |
| Partner Sales Rep | Yes | Must be certified |

### Protection Periods

| Partner Tier | Standard Protection | With Extension Request |
|--------------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Authorized | 30 days | 45 days |
| Select | 45 days | 60 days |
| Premier | 60 days | 90 days |
| Elite | 90 days | 120 days |

### Conflict Resolution

When multiple partners register the same opportunity:
1. First complete registration wins (timestamp-based)
2. If within 4 hours: SDI reviews for completeness and relationship depth
3. Disputes escalated to Regional Partner Director
4. Final decision within 5 business days

## Compliance and Ethics

### Partner Code of Conduct

All partners must adhere to:

1. **Honest Representation** - Accurately represent SDI products and capabilities
2. **Ethical Sales Practices** - No bribery, corruption, or unfair dealing
3. **Customer Privacy** - Protect customer data per SDI standards
4. **Competitive Integrity** - No disparagement, compete on merits
5. **Regulatory Compliance** - Adhere to all applicable laws and regulations
6. **AI Ethics** - Follow SDI's Ethical AI Framework in all customer engagements

### Audit Rights

SDI reserves the right to:
- Audit partner sales records (annual, with 30 days notice)
- Review customer satisfaction data
- Assess certification currency
- Evaluate marketing material compliance

### Termination Provisions

Partnership may be terminated for:
- Material breach of agreement (30-day cure period)
- Failure to meet minimum requirements (2 consecutive quarters)
- Ethical violations (immediate termination possible)
- Bankruptcy or insolvency
- Change of control (60-day review period)
