## Welcome to SDI Benefits

Soong-Daystrom Industries is committed to providing comprehensive benefits that support your health, financial security, and work-life balance. This guide covers all benefits available to eligible employees.

---

## Eligibility

### Full-Time Employees

**Definition**: Employees scheduled to work 30+ hours per week
**Benefits Eligibility**: First day of employment
**Dependent Coverage**: Spouse/domestic partner, children to age 26

### Part-Time Employees

**Definition**: Employees scheduled to work 20-29 hours per week
**Benefits Eligibility**: After 90 days of employment
**Coverage Level**: Prorated based on hours worked

### Contractors and Temporary Workers

Not eligible for benefits. Contact Talent Acquisition for conversion opportunities.

## Enrollment and Changes

### Annual Enrollment

**When**: October 15 - November 15
**Effective**: January 1 of following year

### Qualifying Life Events

You may change benefits within 30 days of:
- Marriage/divorce
- Birth/adoption
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status
- Moving to new coverage area

### How to Enroll

1. Log in to Workday
2. Navigate to Benefits
3. Review and select plans
4. Add/verify dependents
5. Confirm elections
