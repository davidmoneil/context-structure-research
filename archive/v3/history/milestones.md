# Company Milestones

## Timeline of Significant Events

### 2089 - Foundation Year

**March 15, 2089**: Company Founded
- Soong-Daystrom Industries, Inc. incorporated in Delaware
- Founders: Dr. Arik Soong III and Dr. Richard Daystrom Jr.
- Initial funding: $47 million (seed round)

**April 8, 2089**: First Office
- Opened 24,000 sq ft facility in San Mateo, California
- First 12 employees began work

**December 12, 2089**: Series A Funding
- Raised $180 million led by SoftBank Vision Fund
- Valuation: $890 million

### 2090-2094 - Research Phase

**June 15, 2090**: First Positronic Node
- Successfully fabricated first functional positronic neural node
- Breakthrough in synthetic cognition research

**November 8, 2091**: 1,000-Node Network
- Achieved stable operation of 1,000-node positronic network
- Demonstrated basic pattern recognition

**March 22, 2093**: Human-Level Pattern Recognition
- Positronic system exceeded human baseline in visual pattern recognition
- Published landmark paper in Nature

**December 3, 2094**: Mark I Prototype
- Completed first commercial humanoid robot prototype
- Began preparation for CES 2095 launch

### 2095-2099 - First Products

**January 8, 2095**: Mark I Launch
- Unveiled Mark I Assistant at CES 2095
- First orders: 847 units in first week
- Price: $47,000

**June 30, 1995**: First Profitable Quarter
- Q2 2095: First quarter of profitability
- Revenue: $89 million
- Net income: $4.2 million

**September 12, 1997**: Mark II Launch
- Improved cognitive capabilities
- 40% cost reduction
- Price: $28,000

**March 4, 2099**: San Francisco Headquarters
- Moved to current San Francisco headquarters
- 180,000 sq ft campus
- Consolidated all Bay Area operations

### 2100-2109 - Growth Era

**February 17, 2100**: NASDAQ IPO
- Initial public offering at $47 per share
- Raised $2.4 billion
- Market cap at IPO: $12 billion

**August 23, 2102**: First International Expansion
- Opened Tokyo R&D center (47 employees)
- Partnership with Toyota announced

**November 30, 2104**: Mark V Launch
- Final generation of Mark series
- Cumulative Mark series sales: 847,000 units

**May 15, 2105**: Industrial Pivot Announced
- Strategic decision to enter industrial automation
- Acquired Industrial Robotics Solutions for $420 million

**March 8, 2108**: PCS v1 Launch
- Launched Positronic Companion Series
- Revolutionary consumer companion robot
- Established new product category

**October 12, 2108**: Richard Daystrom Jr. Retires
- Co-founder stepped down as Chief Scientist
- Remained on Board of Directors
- Awarded Turing Award same year

### 2110-2119 - Expansion & Transformation

**January 1, 2110**: Arik Soong III Transitions to Chairman
- Dr. Jennifer Wong becomes CEO (2010-2015)
- Strategic focus on consumer products

**July 22, 2112**: Munich R&D Center
- Opened European R&D center
- Focus on industrial automation and safety systems

**April 18, 2114**: SCE Platform Launch
- Launched Synthetic Consciousness Engine
- First commercially available advanced AI platform
- B2B licensing model

**January 15, 2115**: Dr. Maya Chen Becomes CEO
- Fifth CEO in company history
- Focus on neural interfaces and consciousness research

**November 3, 2116**: Austin Manufacturing Center
- Opened largest manufacturing facility
- 1.2 million sq ft
- Capacity: 300,000 units annually

**June 28, 2118**: IAP Platform Launch
- Industrial Automation Platform 1.0
- Entered industrial robotics market
- First year revenue: $2.8 billion

### 2120-Present - Neural & Consciousness Era

**March 15, 2120**: NeuroSync Labs Acquisition
- Acquired leading neural interface company for $8.2 billion
- Added 847 employees
- Gained critical NIM technology

**September 8, 2020**: NIM Launch
- Launched Neural Interface Modules
- FDA approval for medical applications
- Consumer version planned for 2125

**January 22, 2021**: Project Prometheus Initiated
- Board approved major consciousness research program
- $2.4 billion budget over 5 years
- Most ambitious project in company history

**April 12, 2121**: Richard Daystrom Jr. Passes
- Co-founder passed away at age 82
- Memorial service attended by 4,000
- Daystrom Research Fellowship established

**November 8, 2122**: Consciousness Breakthrough
- Project Prometheus achieved first self-referential AI loop
- Consciousness indicator score: 0.45 (first measurement)
- Published limited findings in Nature

**October 15, 2123**: Singapore R&D Campus
- Broke ground on Singapore research campus
- $420 million investment
- Expected completion: 2125

**August 15, 2024**: Quantum Dynamics Acquisition Announced
- Agreement to acquire quantum computing company
- $3.2 billion deal
- Expected close: Q1 2025

**October 24, 2024**: Record Q3 Results
- Revenue: $12.4 billion (best quarter ever)
- Consciousness indicator score reached 0.72
- Raised full-year guidance
